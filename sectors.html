SECTORS

Corporate

In today’s fast changing, complex and increasingly interconnected world, one of the main challenges companies whether large or small face is establishing a deliberate and coherent perception in the minds of their stakeholders. This is why creating a strong corporate brand that speaks with one voice, is clear and reliable is worth every bit of the effort. A company’s reputations is manifested in its ability to bring out a human face to the world and become believable and relatable. Today’s business environment demands open, honest and transparent communications. CALLA PR’s mission is to help you craft, build, sustain and defend your reputation. We understand how to fix ourselves in your shoes and create strategies that first and foremost guard your interests in your spheres of operations.

Real Estate

Real Estate in Kenya is one of the most booming sectors. Public relations is a crucial element to give projects an edge over others through consistent well packaged communication. The question you should ask yourself if you’re in the real estate sector is, ‘’what good is a great project, if no one knows about it?’’ And that’s where we come in. Our team at CALLA PR is experienced in working on mega real estate projects. They have interacted with household names in the sector and industry leaders, gaining a wealth of experience in real estate communication strategies and implementation. Through strategic PR campaigns that incorporate media relations, B2B, stakeholder mapping and influencer engagement, we help put our clients’ real estate projects on the map and publicize their listings. We have extensive experience working with business and real estate editors from a myriad of well-respected media outlets as well as non-traditional media, so we know what constitutes a great story.

Food and Agribusiness

When it comes to food, Africans say it better – Words are sweet, but they never take the place of food. ~Ibo Proverb… and one who eats alone cannot discuss the taste of the food with others. ~African Proverb. For the place food has in our hearts and minds here in Africa, it should be noted that it comes with extensive thoughts. These include nutrition issues, safety, affordability, technology, security, health, culture, sustainability and also culinary advancements. Navigating these topics – and many more – is often the reality of working in the world of food, beverage and agribusiness today. Our team works at the core of these dynamics and partners with our food, beverage and agribusiness clients to make the most of their opportunities and to help address their challenges – from the farm, processing factory to the hearts and minds of consumers and stakeholders.
